# Code of Conduct

## Our Commitment

We are committed to providing a welcoming, inclusive, and harassment-free experience for everyone, regardless of age, body size, disability, ethnicity, gender identity and expression, level of experience, nationality, personal appearance, political beliefs, profession, race, religion, sex, sexual identity and orientation, socioeconomic status, or any other characteristic.

## Expected Behavior

All community members are expected to:

- **Be Respectful**: Treat all people with respect and courtesy
- **Be Inclusive**: Welcome and support people of all backgrounds
- **Be Collaborative**: Work together constructively toward shared goals
- **Be Patient**: Give people the benefit of the doubt
- **Be Professional**: Keep discussions focused on the project and its goals

## Unacceptable Behavior

The following behaviors are considered harassment and are unacceptable:

- Offensive or derogatory comments related to protected characteristics
- Intimidation, threats, or hostile behavior
- Unwanted sexual attention or advances
- Deliberate exclusion or discrimination
- Posting or threatening to post private information
- Other conduct that is clearly inappropriate in a professional context

## Reporting Issues

If you witness or experience unacceptable behavior:

1. **Document it**: Note the specific behavior and context
2. **Report it**: Contact the project maintainers directly
3. **Provide details**: Include relevant information to help us understand

**Report to**: contact email in README

All reports will be treated seriously and confidentially.

## Enforcement

### Consequences

Community members who engage in unacceptable behavior may face:

- Warning and request to stop the behavior
- Temporary or permanent removal from community spaces
- Bans from issue discussions or pull requests
- Public acknowledgment of the violation (at discretion of moderation team)

### Moderation

- Maintainers have the right to edit or remove comments that violate this code
- Maintainers will make good-faith efforts to warn before banning
- Bans are typically permanent but can be appealed after a reasonable period

## Scope

This Code of Conduct applies to:

- All project spaces (GitHub issues, pull requests, discussions)
- Project-related external spaces (conferences, events, online forums)
- Private communications when discussing project matters
